Global Logistics

Seamless freight shipping, documentation compliance, and custom clearance designed to get your commodities delivered safely, on time, and within budget.

End-to-End Sea & Land Freight

We partner with major shipping lines to secure guaranteed vessel space and competitive freight rates. Our logistics network handles port-to-port and door-to-door cargo movement, providing optimal route planning.

  • Full Container Load (FCL) & Less Container Load (LCL)
  • Multi-modal domestic and international transport networks
  • Reliable port operations and loading protocols

Customs Clearance & Trade Compliance

International shipping requires absolute accuracy in export documentation. Our expert in-house customs clearance desk ensures all trade compliance requirements are fully met, eliminating the risk of customs delays or penalties.

  • HSN code categorization & tariff assessments
  • Phytosanitary Certificates & Fumigation Certificates
  • Certificate of Origin processing & Bank documentation
