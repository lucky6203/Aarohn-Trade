Our Products

Premium agricultural commodities sourced directly from the finest producers in India and delivered globally with absolute quality assurance.

Aromatic & Pure Spices

India is world-renowned for its spices, and we source only the highest grade whole and powdered spices. Our collection undergoes stringent laboratory testing to ensure zero contamination, perfect moisture content, and rich essential oil retention.

  • Turmeric (High Curcumin content)
  • Black Pepper (Malabar & Tellicherry grades)
  • Cumin Seeds & Coriander Seeds
  • Red Chili (Guntur, Byadgi, Kashmiri)

Premium Pulses & Lentils

We export a wide variety of protein-rich pulses and lentils, processed in state-of-the-art facilities. Our sorting and grading processes ensure uniform grain size, high purity index, and excellent cooking qualities.

  • Red Lentils (Masoor Dal / Whole Masoor)
  • Chickpeas (Kabuli & Desi Chickpeas)
  • Mung Beans (Green Gram & Split Mung)
  • Pigeon Peas (Toor Dal / Arhar Dal)

Nutritious Seeds & Grains

From the finest long-grain Basmati Rice to high-quality oilseeds, we cater to bulk food industries and global supermarket chains with customized packaging and certified organic and conventional standards.

  • Premium Basmati & Non-Basmati Rice
  • Sesame Seeds (Natural & Hulled Auto-Sorted)
  • Mustard Seeds & Fenugreek Seeds
