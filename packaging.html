Packaging & Compliance

High-grade packaging, strict quality assurance protocols, and custom private labeling to preserve purity, extend shelf life, and comply with target country import regulations.

Preservative & Robust Packaging

Agricultural commodities are prone to moisture damage, insect infestation, and spoilage during transits. We implement robust, food-grade packaging materials specifically designed for international sea-freight containers.

  • Food-grade Jute and PP Woven Bags (25kg / 50kg bulk packs)
  • Multi-layered kraft paper bags for milled powders and flours
  • Vacuum packaging & MAP (Modified Atmosphere) for high-value spices
  • Custom Private Labeling & Retail packing services

Strict Food Safety & Quality Standards

Our quality control protocols extend from harvest sourcing to final container sealing. We follow strict quality control checklists, ensuring every batch meets international standards for cleanliness, moisture parameters, and sizing.

  • SGS / Geo-Chem independent batch inspections
  • Advanced optical sorting and metal-detecting passes
  • Fumigation compliance and moisture content limits (<12%)< /li>
